pH sensor(shown in Sensors\pH_sensor.jpg)

	+ o/p Pin : A0 (Analog Pin of Arduino)
	- o/p Pin : Gnd
	+ 9V Pin : +9V (Seperate Battery to be used)
	- Pin : Gnd

Colour Sensor(shown in Sensors\colour_sensor.jpg)

	S0 : 8 (Digital Pin of Arduino)
	S1 : 9 (Digital Pin of Arduino)
	S2 : 12 (Digital Pin of Arduino)
	S3 : 11 (Digital Pin of Arduino)
	Sout : 10 (Digital Pin of Arduino)
	OE : Gnd
	Gnd : Gnd
	Vcc : +5V (of Arduino)

Temperature Sensor(shown in Sensors\temperature_sensor.jpg)

	Red wire : +5V (of Arduino)
	Black wire : Gnd
	Yellow/white wire : A2 (Analog Pin of Arduino)

Lactometer should be placed in the milk and its reading to be noted.(shown in Sensors\lactometer.png)
